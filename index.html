OVERVIEW

Vision

- To be an innovative company that has a significant impact on the construction industry in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia and to be effective in leading and executing projects in accordance with the most recent global methods, systems, and standards. - To leverage our team's acquired and accumulated experiences to boost a competitive work environment that enhances client satisfaction.

Mission

Our company's involvement in the important field of MEP projects imposes on us a responsibility toward clients through: - Adopting the latest and modern systems in accordance with international standards and codes. - Overcoming obstacles that may negatively affect the project's time and budget. - Collaborating with the client and stakeholders during the project lifecycle to achieve the desired outcome.

Experience

Our company has an integrated work team with extensive knowledge of electrical and mechanical projects, which gives us a competitive edge in the construction industry. This team has spent many years contributing to the success and completion of numerous large private and public sector projects. which greatly enhances the value of our company and contributes to the successful and efficient implementation of more projects in the future.

ABOUT

(UIC) One of the most ambitious and developed companies in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, specializing in MEP works and having gained extensive experience in MEP constructions based on the latest local and international methods, standards, and specifications.

With innovative and practical solutions, we constantly strive to provide the business with more value and innovation than you can imagine.

SERVICES

Internal MEP Systems

UIC has wide experience in executing and maintaining internal MEP systems such as mechanical systems, electrical systems and control.

Infrastructure Works

UIC offers a wide range of infrastructure works such as water, drainage, firefighting, grey water, chilled water, and stormwater networks. We also have vast experience in cable trenches, feeder pillars, street lighting systems, and power substations.

Construction & Civil Works

Our services are extended to cover a wide range of civil works including but not limited to earthworks , insulation, concrete cable trenches , manholes and chambers, and road construction.
